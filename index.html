Terms of Use
⚠️ Not veterinary advice

Everything in this app - breed traits, health notes, and care tips - is general information only, not medical advice, and it can change or vary by individual animal. If you have any concern about your pet's health or behavior, please talk to a licensed veterinarian or another qualified animal professional rather than relying on this app alone.

The "quickest learner" badge reflects a commonly cited, general reference point on obedience and working trainability - not a live or official ranking, and not a measure of overall intelligence.

Photo credits

Sources & disclaimer

The information on each profile - temperament, health watch, care needs, and cost estimates - reflects commonly cited, general reference points pulled together for a quick overview. It's meant as a starting point for research, not a substitute for advice from a vet, breeder, or rescue with direct experience of an individual animal.

Every animal is different. Health conditions, costs, and temperament can vary a lot by individual, line, and location, so treat anything here as a general guide rather than a guarantee.

This app doesn't provide medical advice. If you're concerned about a specific animal's health, please talk to a licensed vet.
