How to Access the App
No downloading needed! The app lives online. Just open the link and you're in.
App Link
The Soccer Tracker by Robvalenz Apps
Step 1 - Open the App
1
Open Chrome on your Android phone.
2
Type or paste this in the address bar: The Soccer Tracker by Robvalenz Apps and hit Enter.
3
The app loads instantly. No sign in or setup needed!
Step 2 - Install to Home Screen
4
Once the app is open, tap the three dots ⋮ in the top right corner of Chrome.
5
Tap "Add to Home Screen" and name it The Soccer Tracker.
6
Tap Add and the ⚽ icon appears on your home screen like a real app!
Step 3 - Next Time
7
Just tap the ⚽ icon on your home screen and the app opens directly in full screen.
8
Scores update automatically. Pull down to refresh if you want the very latest results.
💡
App not loading? Make sure you're using Chrome and that you have an internet connection. Try closing and reopening Chrome if it freezes.
🔄
Scores not updated? Pull down on the screen to refresh and get the very latest results.
How to Clear Your Picks
✓
Easiest way: Go to the Picks tab and tap the red Clear All button. Done instantly.
✓
From Chrome settings: Tap the three dots in Chrome, go to Settings, then Privacy and Security, then Clear Browsing Data. Check Site Data and tap Clear.
🔒 Your Privacy
Like most apps we track how many people visit each day - just a number, nothing personal. We never collect your name, location, or any personal information. Your picks stay on your device only. We don't sell or share any data. Ever.

Step 1 - Open the App
1
Open Safari on your iPhone. It must be Safari for the home screen install to work properly.
2
Type or paste this in the address bar: The Soccer Tracker by Robvalenz Apps and tap Go.
3
The app loads instantly. No sign in or setup needed!
Step 2 - Install to Home Screen
4
Tap the Share button at the bottom of Safari. It looks like a box with an arrow pointing up ↑.
5
Scroll down in the share menu and tap "Add to Home Screen."
6
Name it The Soccer Tracker and tap Add in the top right corner. The ⚽ icon appears on your home screen!
Step 3 - Next Time
7
Just tap the ⚽ icon on your home screen and the app opens in full screen like a real app.
8
Scores update automatically. Pull down to refresh if you want the very latest results.
💡
App not loading? Make sure you're using Safari and not Chrome or Firefox. Only Safari supports Add to Home Screen on iPhone.
🔄
Scores not updated? Pull down on the screen to refresh and get the very latest results.
How to Clear Your Picks
✓
Easiest way: Go to the Picks tab and tap the red Clear All button. Done instantly.
✓
From iPhone settings: Go to Settings on your iPhone, scroll down to Safari, tap Clear History and Website Data. This clears all saved app data from your device.
