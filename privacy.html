Privacy Policy

The Simple Pet Index (by Robvalenz Apps) is a reference app for learning about dog, cat, bird, and reptile breeds. This page explains what information the app does, and does not, collect.

No account, no sign-up

The app doesn't require creating an account, logging in, or providing any personal information to use it.

What's stored, and where

Some features save small pieces of information directly on your own device, using your browser or app's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is never sent to us or anyone else. It includes:

  • Which pets you've marked as favorites
  • Whether you've chosen light or dark mode

You can clear this at any time by clearing the app's storage/cache through your device settings, which resets these preferences.

No analytics, no tracking, no ads

The app does not use analytics tools, advertising networks, or third-party trackers. We don't know who's using the app, how often, or what they look at.

Photos from Wikimedia Commons

Breed photos are loaded from Wikimedia Commons, a third-party image host. Loading these images works like loading any image on the web: your device requests the image directly from Wikimedia's servers, which may log standard technical information (like an IP address) the same way any website does. We don't control or receive this information. You can review Wikimedia's own privacy policy for details on their side.

No data is sold or shared

We don't sell, rent, or share any information, because we don't collect any in the first place beyond what's described above.

Children's privacy

The app doesn't knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children, since it has no accounts, forms, or data collection of any kind.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same page with a new "last updated" date.
